There are four types of forces in this, universe they are:

STRONG NUCLEAR FORCE

WEAK NUCLEAR FORCE

ELECTROSTATIC FORCE

GRAVITATIONAL FORCE

Hadrons: These are particles that feel the STRONG NUCLEAR FORCE and WEAK NUCLEAR FORCE and are made of other particles called QUARKS.

Leptons: These are particles that feel the WEAK NUCLEAR FORCE and are NOT made of anything, in other words they are FUNDAMENTAL PARTICLES.

Quarks:These are FUNDAMENTAL PARTICLES that make up a hadron.

Charged Particles: Any particles that has a POSITIVE(+) or NEGATIVE(-) can attract and repel from each other via an ELECTROSTATIC FORCE.

Anti Particles: These are Particles with the same mass but OPPOSITE CHARGES and QUANTUM PROPERTIES
